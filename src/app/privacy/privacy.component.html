Privacy Policy

Information we collect

We collect email addresses and any other information voluntarily entered into forms on the site.

When you browse the site, we also automatically receive your computer’s internet protocol (IP) address and information about your web browser and operating system.

How we use information we collect

We use your email address to allow you to reset the password of your account.

We use your email address to send notifications about a game you are playing in or are invited to.

We use information about your web browser and operating system to understand how our site is used and to improve our site.

Sharing

We do not sell or otherwise provide to third parties any of this information, except to provide the products and services you’ve requested, with your permission, or as required by law.

We use PayPal for donations. You can learn more about PayPal and read its privacy policy at https://www.paypal.com/myaccount/privacy/privacyhub.

How we protect your information

To protect your personal information, we take reasonable precautions and follow industry best practices to ensure your data is not inappropriately lost, misused, accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed.

Sensitive and private data exchange between our computers and yours happens via SSL secured communication channel and is encrypted and protected with digital signatures.

Your data is stored on Amazon Web Services in Ireland.

Cookies

We do not use cookies or similar techniques.

Your rights

Contact us at info@boardgamefiesta.com to exercise any of the following rights:

  • You can be informed whether we store your data.
  • You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • You can request that we correct any personal data we hold about you - for example your email address.
  • You can request that your account records be erased.

We may need you to provide us with additional information, which may include personal data, to verify your identity and the nature of your request.

Note that erasure of your account data may interfere with your ability to use the service.
